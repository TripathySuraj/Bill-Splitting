Why to choose Split & Happy

User friendly GUI(front end) with accurate and uniform designing that is all similar functionalities should of same design so that it does not confuses the clients or users. This application is based on bill splitting or exchanging money. In this application there will be a Super Admin and only he/she will be able to create new groups or setting group admin if group has not any. Also in a group only admin could add new members or delete a member from the group. Also inside the group people can lend money to each other but all the transaction details will be extracted fromthe image of the bill so that no fraud can occur. Also new members will first have to Sign up and fill their details. And then in case the new member wants to forma new group then Super Admin will make the new group and make the new member its admin. And for already registered member he/she just needs to fill the required login credentials to enter the application. Also net amount are also maintained for each member in the database.​
